Who I am

The original full stack, remote contractor.

I started coding when I was 15 years old. My dad was a programmer and asked me to help out on various projects. After meeting with customers and gathering requirements most work was completed at home. It was usually a full stack setup starting with good database design, some code, forms, and reports added on top. This was pre-internet so armed with our floppy disks back to the customers we would go.

Then came the internet, the dot com boom, and bust and I never looked back. I worked on various contracts in and around London and enjoyed working with the people I met. After a brief dabble with classic ASP along came ASP.NET which together with many other tools I've enjoyed working with ever since.

I believe in Rapid Application Development where building robust software quickly is the key to success. I am proud of what I do and respect values such as:

  • Integrity and Honesty
  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Collaboration and Respect
  • Desire to share knowledge, achievements, and experiences
  • Care about my colleagues and their quality of life
